From Argentina to Italy

Horacio Pagani is the Argentine-Italian founder of Pagani Automobili, and Italian auto-maker best known for its hypercars and manufacture of carbon fiber. Pagani was born in Casilda, Argentina to Luca and Maria Pagani. Luca was a baker while Maria was a painter. At an early age Pagani took an interest in engineering while still living in Argentina, where he started working with fiberglass, and then began working with on a Limitada Santafisna race car. Although he had no background in mechanical engineering, Pagani was capable of building a working car with a Renault engine.
