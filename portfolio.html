Pagani Automobili

Although the Countach Evoluzione never made it into production, Pagani saw the potential of carbon fiber in cars, so he took his autoclave and left Lamborghini to start his own production company. Pagani became one of the best carbon fiber manufacturers rivaled only by McLaren and NASA as he was able to produce this material efficiently and many racing teams used his service to produce parts for cars and motorcycles. Pagani decided to design his own car and after seven years, he revealed his first work of art, the Zonda, made mainly from carbon fiber and a V12 engine from Mercedes. Pagani made several versions of the Zonda, and although they shared the same name, most of them were different as the company always improved each version to create the best car possible that carries elegant styling.
